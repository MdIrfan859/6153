HISTORY & CULTURE:

The Burj Khalifa is known world wide for its
astounding height of 828 meters which is
currently the tallest building in the world.
Standing at such heights the building has held
this record since its completion in 2010.

The Burj Khalifa was under construction for 6
years, beginning in 2004 and finished in 2010.
Just a few days later, on January 4,2010,
the building was officially opened to the public.
Originally named Burj Dubai, it was renamed
Burj Khalifa to honor the ruler of Abu Dhabi
and the President of the United Arab Emirates.
